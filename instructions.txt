Kanamutti
* There are 6 levels in the game.
* You should find winning pot from the displaying pots.
* In each level you have 3 chances.
* - If you found winning pot in your first chance you got 30 marks
  - If you found winning pot in your second chance you got 20 marks
  - If you found winning pot in your third chance you got 10 marks
* When level upping number of displaying pots are incease.

Lissana Gaha
* The tree height is 33ft
* There are two buttons (Left & Right)
* When grease drops are comes down, you should survive form them by moving left and right using buttons.
* When you go up, grease droping speed is incease.
* When you go 1ft up, you got 1 Mark.
